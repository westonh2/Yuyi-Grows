Yuyi_grows

Yuyi_grows is a plant nursery that specializes in rare and unusual tropical plants focusing on carnivorous plants.

About Yuyi

Yuyi_grows started as a single Nepenthes sanguinea and a handful of posts on Instagram. Over the years, the set ups scaled up as the number of plants outgrew the previous grow spaces. This evolved from a sunny windowsill to a lighted terrarium to currently a tropical greenhouse. We are committed to bringing the wonderful world of carnivorous plants to our customers and growing this community.
